Notice to http://rsb.info.nih.gov/ visitors:
The "ROI Manager Tools" toolset is now distributed with BAR, a curated
collection of "Broadly Applicable Routines" for ImageJ[1].

The easiest way to install the Toolset is to subscribe to the BAR update
site[2]. Alternatively, the file can also be downloaded from Github[3].

[1] http://imagej.net/BAR
[2] http://imagej.net/BAR#Installation
[3] https://github.com/tferr/Scripts/tree/master/BAR/src/main/resources/tools
